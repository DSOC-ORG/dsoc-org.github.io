What is the process of working on demand ?

1. Communicate your software problem with us.

2. Let us analyse your problem ( in agreed time interval, we can sign non disclosure agreement ).

3. We negotiate the price, the time, and the defintion of done.

4. You pay us upon delivery.

What is the process of working long term ?

This applies if you want to start building your project with us, or you want to transfer the maintenance of an existing one.

1. Start an open negitiation about your problems and wanted terms.

2. Let us analyse your problem (we can sign non disclosure agreement).

3. We discuss the results of analysis and terms.

4. If we agree, we can sign a short contract ( up to 3 months ) with cancel anytime policy, to assure you're satisfied with agreement and execution.

5. If the short contracts goes well, and you are satisfied with our services, we can sign contracts for longer time periods.
